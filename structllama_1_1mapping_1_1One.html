template<typename T_ArrayDomain, typename T_DatumDomain>
struct llama::mapping::One< T_ArrayDomain, T_DatumDomain >

Maps all ArrayDomain coordinates into the same location and layouts struct members consecutively. This mapping is used for temporary, single element views.
